Harvey Memories Project (Coming in Spring, 2018)

The Harvey Memories project is a community-driven digital archive that collects stories, images, audio, and video about Hurricane Harvey and its aftermath. By enabling people to share their artifacts and memories, the Texas Harvey Archive aims to help the public make sense of the storm and to support researchers and students in seeking to understand the hurricane’s impact.
